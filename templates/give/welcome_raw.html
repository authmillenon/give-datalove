give.datalove — the new way to show your datalove

Welcome to give.datalove.

This website enables you to give and receive datalove from other people all over the world. Even though you don't know them, they can share their love with you.

After you have registered you can install a little widget at your website or blog to share the datalove.

We hope you have as much fun seeing the datalove grow, as we had creating this site.
